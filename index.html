IT student with a passion for cloud computing, cybersecurity, and system engineering !


Hello! I’m Nisal Fernando, a passionate and dedicated undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology (BICT). I'm on a focused journey to become a Cloud Security Engineer, blending my interests in cloud computing, networking, cybersecurity, and IT system engineering. My foundation in IT began with diploma qualifications in Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking, English and ICT, and further enriched through Microsoft and Cisco certifications. These experiences have given me a strong base in areas like cloud infrastructure (Azure), network security, ethical hacking (Kali Linux), and core IT systems. Currently, I’m expanding my knowledge through university-level courses in programming (C, Java), mathematics, physics, and communication skills—essential for both technical and professional growth.

Beyond academics, I’m a curious learner who values discipline, self-improvement, and meaningful tech solutions.
